Star Wars universe

Star wars in short is all about the epic fight about the forces of good and evil. It has 3 main timelines in its narrative.

First one is about Hans, Leah and Luke and their adventures in defeating the evil emperor and his evil subordinate Darth Vader.

Second one is about the story of young Anakin Skywalker and how he was initially good and the how he fell into the dark side.

Third and most recent one is about Rey who is the daughter of Luke and here quest to bring balance to the force.
